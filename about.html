Our Story

Inspired by Melbourne’s love of great food, we celebrate Italian traditions with a modern touch.

La Trobe University Restaurant was created as a welcoming gathering place for students, staff, and the local community. Our menu is rooted in Italian cuisine—simple, generous, and seasonal. From slow-simmered sauces to hand-stretched pizza dough, we craft everything with care.

Atmosphere

Family-friendly and relaxed, with warm lighting and comfortable seating. Whether it’s a quick lunch between classes or a leisurely dinner with friends, we’ve designed a space for good conversation and great food.

Meet the Team

Our kitchen team is led by our head chef, who brings years of experience from trattorias across Australia. Front-of-house staff are trained to make every guest feel at home.
